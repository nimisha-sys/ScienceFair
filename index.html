Logic & Music Experiment

Welcome to Our Research Study

This experiment investigates how different types of background music affect logical reasoning performance.

What to Expect

  • You will be randomly assigned to one of three music conditions
  • Complete 10 logic questions in 2 minutes. It's ok if you cannot finish all questions.
  • Some questions are multiple choice, others require text answers
  • Music (if assigned) will play during the survey
  • The survey will auto-submit when time expires

Privacy Statement

Your data is protected:

  • Used only for research purposes
  • No commercial use of any kind
  • No financial information collected
  • No credential information collected
  • Participation is voluntary and anonymous
